Biography

My name is Jixiang Yu. I am a senior undergraduate student, and pursuing my B.Sc. degree in Computer Science with DUFE. I joined Assoc. Prof. Ming Gao's lab in my sopomore year as a research assistant. With the guidance of Prof. Gao, I participated in several researches mainly related to deep learning methods (CNN, Transformer, GNN) and their applications in industrial scenarios(e.g. workload prediction of cloud workflow). I am very fortunate to have opportunities to cooperate with Prof. Ip and Prof Yung of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, and to join the project where Prof. Gao cooperated with Prof Qian of Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

After obtaining my B.Sc. degree, I will join Function Hub, The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology(Guangzhou Campus) in Sep, 2022 as a Ph.D. student, co-supervised by Prof. Hongyuan Liu and Prof. Jiang Xu. During my Ph.D. study, I will focus on machine learning applications in computer architecture design.

I participated in several interdisciplinary projects subject to cloud environment, blood mass spectrometry, and transportation simulation. You can find them in my CV.
