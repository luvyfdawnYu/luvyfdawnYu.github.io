Biography

My name is Jixiang Yu. I am an incoming PhD student of the Department of Computer Science, City University of Hong Kong, supervised by Prof. Ka-Chun Wong. I obtained my bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Technology at Dongbei University of Finance and Economics, supervised by Prof. Ming Gao.

In my undergraduate study, I participated in several researches mainly related to deep learning methods (CNN, Transformer, GNN) and their applications in industrial scenarios(e.g. workload prediction of cloud workflow). I am very fortunate to have opportunities to cooperate with Prof. Ip and Prof. Yung of The Hong Kong Polytechnic University, and to join the project where Prof. Gao cooperated with Prof. Qian of Shanghai Jiao Tong University.

During my PhD study, I will focus on machine learning applications in bioinformatics. I'm also looking for collaborators in Reinforcement Learning and Deep Learning (e.g., computer vision/graph neural networks)

I participated in several interdisciplinary projects subject to cloud environment, blood mass spectrometry, and transportation simulation. You can find them in my CV.
